Contributors are asked to follow the *METh* house-style; download the [Style Sheet](style_sheet_2.pdf) here.  

The language of publication is usually English, and though primary texts should normally be quoted in their original language, translations should be supplied for quotations from all other languages, including French and Latin.

Where possible, *METh* is happy to include illustrations. However, it is the author's responsibility to obtain written permission to reproduce these, in both print and electronic publication, from the private individual, library, or other organisation holding the copyright. Any photographs or other illustrations submitted to *METh* should be accompanied, where appropriate, by a copy of this written permission. A **form of application** for the necessary rights can be downloaded [here](image_copyright.pdf). Payment of copyright fees is the responsibility of the contributor.  

Images should be well-focussed and sharp, preferably as TIFFs at 600dpi.

Contributions for consideration should be sent as e-mail attachments, preferably in a recent version of Word, to one of the Editors:
