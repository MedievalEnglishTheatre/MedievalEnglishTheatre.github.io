Please note that membership runs by the calendar year: that is, you should renew your membership on or near 1 January each year. This will cover the membership rate for the METh Meeting for that year, and if you have opted for it, the journal for that year. This year's journal is Volume 43 (2021).  

To apply, please download and complete the [APPLICATION FORM](apply_2026.pdf), and send this form, enclosing evidence
of payment, as an e-mail attachment to the Acting Treasurer, Dr Mark Chambers: mark.chambers@durham.ac.uk
